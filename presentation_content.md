## Slide 2: What is Word Processing?
**Definition:**
- Software for input, editing, formatting, and output of text.
**Features:**
- Create, edit, save, print.
- Formatting (Bold, Italic, Font).
- Tables and Graphics.
- Spell Check.
**Uses:**
- Letters, Resumes, Reports.

e

## Slide 3: Mail Merge
**Definition:**
- Feature to create multiple documents from a single template and a data source.
**Components:**
1. **Main Document:** The template (e.g., letter body).
2. **Data Source:** List of recipients (e.g., Excel file).
3. **Merged Document:** The final output.

---

## Slide 4: Key Definitions
- **Format Painter:** Copies formatting from one text to another.
- **Header/Footer:** Text at the top/bottom of every page.
- **Endnote/Footnote:** References/notes at the end of document or page.

---

## Slide 5: More Definitions
- **Table:** Grid of rows and columns.
- **Word Art:** Stylized text effects.
- **Drop Cap:** Large initial letter.
- **Watermark:** Faint background text/image.
- **Bookmark:** Saved location for quick access.
